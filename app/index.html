Experience Untaggable Confort

Venice is a sandal-like shoe suitable to be used without socks and comfortable enough to walk miles and miles in a day. A smart creation with singular, cool look suitable to be combined with different clothing styles.

01. Features

Key Points

  • No socks, no sweat and no overheating.
  • Suitable for long walking distances. Guaranteed comfort.
  • Cool looking shoe. Versatile enough to be combined with different styles: from citizen to casual.
  • Machine Washable in cold temperature.
  • Thicker sole & insole: gaining an extra inch.
  • Two positions: wear it closed or as a clog.
  • Deep R&D during conceptualization.

01. Features

A Smart Creation

The Institute of Biomechanics of Valencia (IBV) has been involved throughout the project’s development, to make Venice breathable, comfortable and adjusted to global fitting.

BUILT TO ENDURE

Every part of your Venice has been submitted to exhaustive tests and studies to ensure maximum quality.

  • Premium materials like hand dyed linen, washable smooth leather perforated to maximize perspiration, extra thick leather with raw, natural edges, etc.
  • Ergonomic insole molded in high density Eva and lined with evergreen cow suede.
  • Rubber sponge outsole, extra thick and with its peculiar Venice design in the base.

01. Features

A Wash-N-Walk Smart Creation

Fit on. Walk on. Wash on: Venice is machine washable in cold to leave them as good as new anytime you wish.

Wash them in a cold and gentle cycle (max. 24ºC/75,2ºF) Do not use the tumble dryer and avoid drying them directly in the sun.
